Education

Education for children is important to us, so we strive to provide educational opportunities for communities where students cannot attend school in-person due to special circumstances or events that have affected them. In the image above, we provided refurbished laptops to local schools located in Kenya to assist in elementary education.

Telemedicine

We provide telemedicine to those who are located in areas where access to healthcare is limited and open up gateway for more healthcare opportunities to those in need. A woman from Bosnia can be seen from the image bring provided a checkup exam with the assistance of an on-site nurse and a translator found on the screen where we provide internet for that to be possible.

Job Opportunity

Technology is moving at a extremely fast pace, which also means there are more job opportunities. We provide communities with the technology needed to keep up with the lastest news as well as increased job opportunites for those individuals. We host monthly career fairs in diverse communities, allowing everyone to explore more job opportunities as well volunteering opportunites with our organization.
